Privacy Policy

Still — Gentle Mood Journaling

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Your privacy matters

Still is designed to be a quiet, personal space for reflection. Your thoughts belong to you — not to us.

Data storage

All journal entries, mood logs, and reflections are stored locally on your device.

This data never leaves your phone and is never accessible by us.

Data collection

Still does not collect or store:

  • Journal content
  • Mood entries
  • Personal notes
  • Names, email addresses, or account information

Analytics

Still uses Google Analytics to understand anonymous app usage, such as:

  • App launches
  • General feature usage
  • Interaction frequency

This information is aggregated and does not include personal reflections or identifiable user data.

Data sharing

Still does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Data security

Because your reflections remain on your device, you stay in full control of your data at all times.

Children’s privacy

Still does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
